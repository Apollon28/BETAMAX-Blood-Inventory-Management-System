GENERAL FAQS

WHAT IS BETAMAX?

BETAMAX is a comprehensive Blood Bank Management System designed to facilitate the secure and organized management of blood donations and supplies. It features donor registration, blood donation scheduling, inventory tracking, secure user authentication, and blood request processing.

WHO CAN USE BETAMAX?

BETAMAX is designed for use by blood banks, hospitals, donors, and patients in need of blood transfusions. It serves as a centralized platform to streamline the blood donation and request process.

DONOR INFORMATION

HOW DO I REGISTER AS A DONOR?

To register as a donor, go to the "Donor" section on the BETAMAX homepage, fill out the required personal and medical information, and submit the form. You will receive a confirmation email with further instructions.

HOW CAN I SCHEDULE A BLOOD DONATION?

Once registered, log in to your BETAMAX account, navigate to the "Schedule Donation" section, select a convenient date and time, and confirm your appointment.

CAN I TRACK MY DONTAION HISTORY?

Yes, BETAMAX provides a "Donation History" section where you can view all your past donations, including dates and types of blood donated.

BLOOD REQUEST INFORMATION

HOW DO I REQUEST THROUGH BETAMAX>?

To request blood, log in to your BETAMAX account, navigate to the "Request" section, select the required blood type and quantity, add it to your cart, and proceed to fill out the request form with patient and hospital details.

HOW DO I KNOW IF THE BLOOD TYPE I NEED IS AVAILABLE?

The "Blood Inventory" section displays real-time availability of different blood types. You can see the number of units available for each blood type before making a request.

WHAT INFORMATION DO I NEED TO PROVIDE TO REQUEST BLOOD?

You need to provide patient information (name, age, gender, medical condition), blood type and quantity required, hospital/clinic details, preferred date and time, and your contact information.
